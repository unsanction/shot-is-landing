# Design System

## Overview

SHOT.IS is an ultra-dark, power-core digital studio brand with a militant aesthetic — full black canvas with massive Syne ultra-bold typography in white and a single crimson red (#FF1100) accent. Layout alternates between black (dark, god-mode sections) and white (roster/character showcase), creating high-contrast chapter breaks. The brand language is confrontational and maximalist: 140px headings, AI character identities treated as legends, scrolling marquees of "READY / PERFECT / VIRAL" as a power mantra.

## Colors

- **Primary Surface**: `#000000` — full black for hero, stats, marquee, and CTA sections
- **Deep Surface Alt**: `#0D0D0D`, `#111111`, `#050505` — near-black for layered depth and footer
- **Primary Content**: `#FFFFFF` — white for all hero and dark-bg text
- **Accent Red**: `#FF1100` — singular crimson for the "Join the Inner Circle" CTA section, buttons, and brand heat
- **Light Surface**: `#E5E7EB` — light gray used in roster/character section backgrounds
- **Roster BG**: `#FFFFFF` — white for the character card sections (contrast chapter)

## Typography

- **Display**: Syne, 800-900 weight. All headings at extreme sizes (96-140px). All caps. No letter-spacing adjustments — the weight alone carries authority.
- **Body**: Syne, regular weight. Subheadings and descriptor lines at 16-20px. Sentence case, minimal.
- **Labels**: Syne, mono-style usage (KAI_OS, VEXA-9, LUNA_CORE) — underscore naming convention for AI character identities.

## Elevation

Flat and brutal. No glassmorphism, no card borders, no shadows. Depth is created purely through: (1) radial gradient glows behind key elements (deep red/purple glow behind hero sphere), (2) section color alternation (black → white → black), (3) scale contrast between 140px hero text and 16px body copy. The only "glow" effect is the atmospheric orb in the hero — a dark maroon radial gradient suggesting a planet or lens flare.

## Components

- **Power Sphere Hero**: Full-bleed dark video background with a large radial glow orb (deep red-maroon gradient), massive centered headline, short descriptor, and outlined white button
- **God-Mode Title**: H2 at 128px, left-aligned, all-caps, zero decoration — raw typographic dominance
- **AI Character Film Cells**: Horizontal scrolling roster cards — portrait image + name (h3, 36px, 900wt) + descriptor tag line (monospace convention)
- **Stat Grid**: 2×2 grid of large numbers (847M+, 340%) with label + sublabel, white on black
- **Viral Marquee**: Infinite horizontal scroll of "READY · PERFECT · VIRAL" at 96px, all-caps, white on black — the brand's battle cry
- **Crimson CTA Block**: Full-width `#FF1100` red section for "JOIN THE INNER CIRCLE" with access-gate framing

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use #FF1100 sparingly — it's the single power accent, not a tint
- Keep typography massive, blunt, and all-caps for headings
- Use the radial red-maroon glow for atmospheric depth behind key elements
- Honor the black/white section alternation as a structural chapter device
- Treat AI character names with underscore convention (VEXA-9, KAI_OS)

### Don'ts
- Do not use gradients on text — white and black only on type
- Do not round corners on buttons beyond minimal pill shape
- Do not use multiple accent colors — the palette is black, white, and #FF1100 only
- Do not animate at high frequency — power comes from weight, not speed
- Do not use thin weights — minimum 700, prefer 800-900
