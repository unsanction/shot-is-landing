# Campaign Brief

## Product

SHOT.IS is positioned as a high-output AI creator studio for brands that need viral visual content without renting human influencer attention. The site promise centers on reach, creator ownership, and visual dominance.

## Audience

- Growth teams testing paid social creative.
- Founders and brand operators tired of influencer reshoots, schedules, and licensing.
- Creative directors who want controllable AI personas instead of one-off generated assets.

## Brand Rules Used

- Black canvas, white typography, single crimson accent `#FF1100`.
- Heavy all-caps display typography.
- Brutal, direct language.
- No soft SaaS gradients, no friendly mascots, no rounded card system.

## Creator Personas

- `VEXA-9` - cyber-high-end influencer, aspirational, polished, sharp.
- `KAI_OS` - digital rebel, trend saboteur, aggressive creative energy.
- `LUNA_CORE` - virtual oracle, high-concept video identity, enigmatic.

## Ad Hooks

- "847M+ eyes."
- "Zero human influencers."
- "Your next influencer does not sleep."
- "Stop renting faces. Own the creator."
- "Launch the character. Feed the machine."

## Deliverables

- One 22s brand manifesto ad.
- One 15s conversion ad.
- One 10s hook-loop ad.
- Local voiceover scripts and generated audio pipeline.
- Sora prompt specs for optional generated cinematic footage.
