# Design System

## Overview

SHOT.IS is a cyberpunk AI creator agency with maximum attitude. The visual language is dark, aggressive, and high-contrast — jet black canvases slashed with acid red (#FF1100) and pure white. Typography dominates: Syne 900 weight headlines fill frames at 96–140px. The brand alternates between two worlds — black void sections and a singular blood-red CTA section — with no gradual transitions, just hard cuts. Motion is kinetic, not atmospheric.

## Colors

- **Primary Surface**: `#000000` — the void; default background for all hero and content sections
- **Deep Black**: `#050505` / `#0D0D0D` / `#111111` — layered dark surfaces for depth without contrast
- **Primary Content**: `#FFFFFF` — all main typography, borders, UI elements
- **Accent Red**: `#FF1100` — the only accent; CTAs, logo mark, energy moments; use sparingly for max impact
- **Soft Gray**: `#E5E7EB` — secondary text, muted labels
- **White Section**: `#FFFFFF` — the Roster section inverts to full white with black type

## Typography

- **Display**: Syne 800–900. All major headings. Massive scale (96–140px on 1920px canvas). All-caps, tight tracking. The brand voice.
- **Mono**: JetBrains Mono 400–700. Labels, data readouts, nav links, subheadings. 11–14px body, all-caps for accent labels.
- **Sizing hierarchy**: H1 hero: 140px / H2 sections: 96–128px / H3 cards: 36px / Body: 16–18px mono

## Elevation

Flat over void — no shadows, no glassmorphism. Depth comes from scale contrast: massive type behind mid-scale images behind small mono labels. The white Roster section creates the only elevation break — a hard section inversion. The red CTA section is the terminus: pure #FF1100, no overlays.

## Components

- **Void Hero**: Full-bleed black with floating deep-red/purple radial glow sphere. Massive H1 text overlaid. Film-cell pause icons scattered as decoration.
- **Roster Grid**: 3-column character grid on white background — each card a B&W editorial portrait with H3 name + mono descriptor below.
- **Visual Overload Marquee**: Horizontal ticker scrolling "PERFECT / VIRAL / READY" on black — pure kinetic type, no imagery.
- **Stat Counters**: 4-up grid — giant number + small mono label. Numbers dominate. Clean, no decoration.
- **Blood CTA Block**: Full #FF1100 panel. Giant SHOT.IS watermark type in near-black (#0D0D0D). Single button, white outline.
- **Nav Strip**: Top bar with SHOT.IS logo (cyan mark + white wordmark) + mono nav links right-aligned.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use maximum type weight (800–900) — anything lighter reads as weak on this brand
- Hard cut between sections — no crossfades between the black world and white world
- Let the accent red (#FF1100) arrive as a shock moment — one beat, maximum power
- Use all-caps for every headline — lowercase feels off-brand

### Don'ts
- Do not add gradients or glassmorphism — this brand is flat void
- Do not use the red as a background texture or overlay — it's only a terminus/shock color
- Do not animate text slower than 0.3s — this brand is fast and aggressive
- Do not use imagery in color — all character photos are desaturated/B&W
